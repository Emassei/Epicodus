As a simple baseline calculation, you can assume 2 weeks of paid vacation per year. That leaves 50 working weeks, and if you work a typical 40 hours a week, then you have a total of 2,000 hours of work each year. In this case, you can quickly compute the hourly wage by dividing the annual salary by 2000. Your yearly salary of $40,000 is then equivalent to an average hourly wage of $20 per hour.
